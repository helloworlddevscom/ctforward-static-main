About

CT Forward is an organization established to help parents and supporters learn more about Connecticut elected officials, candidates, and committees who support equal access to great public schools for all kids - including public charter schools - and allow these members an easy way to contribute directly to their campaigns.

Our members are parents, educators, and their allies - who all believe that every child, from every community, deserves the opportunity to go to a great public school.

CT Forward is a membership-based, online platform that will help members advocate for issues. For a small, annual dues payment, members can learn more about candidates’ voting records, biography, and be able to contribute to candidates who have stood up for quality public schools in Connecticut’s highest need communities - including public charter schools.
